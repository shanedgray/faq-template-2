  • What is Clinch?

    Clinch is a next generation SaaS recruitment solution to help companies build & manage their employer brand, position themselves as an attractive employer even before they have jobs, promote jobs when positions open up and make better hiring decisions with your team. Clinch helps you reach out to and attract the best talent using job sites, social media and your own web presence.

  • What are the benefits of using Clinch if we are not hiring yet?

    An employer brand takes time to build, start before you are hiring to reap significant benefits later… Companies with a strong employer brand deliver a 50% lower cost per hire, enjoy 28% lower employee turnover and are 5 times more likely to report high employee engagement and that’s just for starters. A strong brand tells a clear story about “why candidates should work here”.

  • How can using Clinch help my company?

    Using Clinch, you create a stylish, mobile-friendly careers site that allows candidates to apply from all devices in less than ten minutes. With a single line of code, you can embed your “team”, “about us” and “careers” pages on your corporate website to allow easy ongoing management and updating of your content and employer brand.


    You can quickly add and promote your jobs for free with one-click posting to our partners in Simply Hired and Recruit.net, and we’ve also included scheduled automated posting to social media channels including Twitter, Facebook and LinkedIn to drive your social recruiting efforts. Analytics help target the channels that work best.


    We’ve partnered with Slack for real time updates on jobs and applications to all your team. Clinch’s applicant tracking helps avoid spreadsheets so you can use your entire team to help find the perfect hire.


    As soon as the applications start arriving, everything you need to know about a candidate is presented in a single view including resume, cover letter, your notes and responses. We’ve even included automatic discovery of social media profiles and the ability to easily engage with candidates.

  • How about some facts and figures?

    Reduced time to hire, improved candidate experience, lower hiring costs and a better candidate fit.
