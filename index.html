  • What is Clinch?

    Clinch’s talent acquisition platform helps companies create effective employer brands to attract and manage applications from the right candidates. From showcasing your workplace to sharing anecdotes and insights from members of your team, Clinch helps you communicate everything that’s great about working with your company to help you attract the right people.

  • Which recruitment challenges does Clinch address?

    Finding and hiring the right people can be difficult and time consuming even when you have a large budget. It’s even harder for early stage companies who are competing with established corporations for the top talent they need to grow their business. It get’s worse, most companies hire “reactively” by waiting until they have an open position. They use jobs boards, word of mouth and social media to try and find a candidate. With no employer brand and little online collateral available to answer …“Why would I want to work with you?” ...applicants from the right candidates are slow to arrive. Desperate to fill positions, companies resort to expensive external recruiters. This increases costs and also results in candidate mismatches causing churn and disruption down the road. “Strong employer brands deliver a 50% lower cost per hire” Growing companies who use Clinch adopt a pro-active approach to hiring from the outset. Using Clinch they create dynamic and engaging careers, team and about us pages to communicate everything that’s great about working for their company to prospective candidates. Even when they are not actively hiring they continually add to their candidate pool and build relationships with the future people they want to hire. Starting the conversation earlier makes the hiring process faster, easier and cheaper and their strong employer brand increases staff engagement and reduces churn. “Candidates we attracted using Clinch were of a far higher standard”

  • What exactly can I do with Clinch?

    · Start to build your employer brand using branded career pages, engaging photos, team information and open jobs in minutes. · Create your own talent network by engaging with the people you want to hire in the future. · Post jobs to major job boards with one click and reach millions of potential candidates every day · Use scheduled social network posting to reach job seekers on Facebook, LinkedIn and Twitter. · Keep track of and respond to all candidates, applicants, prospects and recruits from one platform · Use analytics to make data-driven decisions & drive better outcomes.

  • How about some facts and figures?

    Reduced time to hire, improved candidate experience, lower hiring costs and a better candidate fit.

  • What is the Clinch Startup program?

    Clinch StartUp is a program for Accelerators, Incubators and Co-working spaces (“Lead Company”). Clinch gives all participants access to our talent acquisition platform at 100% discount for the duration of the program.

  • What does the incubator, accelerator or co-working space get?

    As a Lead Company an accelerator, incubator and co-working space can avail of the following; · Clinch Talent Acquisition platform Enterprise package. · A professional photo-shoot of your working space to showcase the environment you work in.n. · Clinch Company profiles – A professionally built Clinch profile for the Incubator and each member company for automatic inclusion on the Lead Company’s website – no more chasing for logos & content from your teams · Clinch Jobs Board – An automated listing of all open jobs in member companies for easy inclusion on your website in jobs board format. · Support from the Clinch team to get you up and running. · Option to extend the offer to your alumni companies · A great perk to offer your Member Companies as follows…

  • What does the Startup company get?

    A Member Company is a startup that is participating in the Lead Company program. They get the following benefits; · Clinch Talent Acquisition platform Standard package. – 100% discount for the duration of the program. · Clinch Company profiles – A professionally built Clinch profile for the member company for automatic inclusion on the Lead Company’s website. · Ability to fill out their profile using the professional shots taken of the workplace. · Support from the Clinch team to get them live.
